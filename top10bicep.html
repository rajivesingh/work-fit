Top 5 Bicep Mass Exercises

Barbell-Curl

  • Holding the barbell with your hands just outside of your hips
  • Keep your chest up and your elbows tight
  • Move bar raising your hands slightly so that you can feel your biceps
  • Keeping the same grip curl the bar up to shoulder height
  • give your biceps a nice at the top squeeze.
  • Start to lower the bar slowly
  • repeat 8-10 times

Dumbbell Curl

  • Stand comfortably straighten the back, keep the head steady.
  • Curl the weight upward toward the chest, breathing out Moving only forearms
  • Hold at the top for few seconds
  • slowly return the arms to the lower resting position
  • repeat 10-12 times

Hammer Curl

  • Hold a pair of dumbbells in your hands
  • Keeping your elbows close to your body
  • Slowly curl the dumbbell up to your shoulders
  • Pause for a second at the top
  • Squeeze your biceps
  • Lower the weights under control
  • repeat 10-12 times

Concentration Curl

  • Sit on a bench first with your feet flat on the floor
  • Pick up a dumbbell in your right hand
  • Place the back of your upper right arm on the inner part of your right thigh
  • Your arm should be extended holding the weight
  • Slowly curl the weight up, only moving your forearms
  • At the top of the move, pause for a while and squeeze your biceps
  • Slowly lower the weight back to the start.
  • repeat 8-10 times

EZ-Bar Curl

  • Hold an EZ-bar in an underhand grip
  • With your arms extended and then curl the bar up towards your chest
  • Pause and squeeze at the top
  • Slowly lower the weight back
  • Repeat 10-12 times
